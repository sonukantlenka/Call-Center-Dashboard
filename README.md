# Call-Center-Dashboards
This repository contains three-dashboard Power BI report built on telecom dataset, presenting a unified dataset through three distinct business lenses—operations, customer retention, and risk. Each dashboard is designed to address specific stakeholder questions while drawing from the same underlying data source.

#  Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard

This dashboard showcases a **Customer Churn Analysis Dashboard** built using Power BI to analyze customer behavior, identify churn patterns, and support data-driven retention strategies.

The dashboard provides a 360° view of:
* Customer demographics
* Account and billing information
* Services usage
* Churn risk indicators
---

##  Key Metrics
* **Customers at Risk:** 1869
* **Technical Tickets (Churn):** 2173
* **Admin Tickets (Churn):** 885
* **Yearly Charges:** 2.86M
* **Monthly Charges:** 139.13K
* **Avg Monthly Charges:** 64.76
---

##  Dashboard Sections
###  Customer Profile
* Gender distribution of customers
* Churn comparison by gender
* Partner vs non-partner ratio
* Senior citizen segmentation
---

###  Account Information
* Average monthly charges analysis
* Paperless billing adoption
* Payment method breakdown:
  * Electronic check
  * Mailed check
  * Bank transfer
  * Credit card
* Customer distribution insights
---

###  Services Subscribed
* Internet services:
  * Fiber optic
  * DSL
  * No internet
* Additional services:
  * Phone service
  * Streaming TV
  * Device protection
  * Online backup
  * Tech support
---

##  Key Insights
* Customers using **fiber optic internet** show higher churn trends
* **Electronic check users** have higher churn rates
* Customers with **higher monthly charges** are more likely to churn
* **Senior citizens and non-partner customers** show different churn behavior patterns

#  Customer Risk Analysis Dashboard

<img width="890" height="510" alt="Customer risk analysis" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6223bda1-5689-4043-96b5-2df6ba9ad96d" />


This dashboad contains a **Customer Risk Analysis Dashboard** designed to identify, analyze, and monitor customers who are at risk of churn.

The dashboard enables businesses to:
* Understand churn behavior
* Analyze customer segments
* Identify high-risk groups
* Improve retention strategies

---

##  Key Metrics
* **Total Customers:** 7043
* **Churn Rate:** 26.54%
* **Total Charges:** 16.06M
* **Customers at Risk:** 1869
* **Total Admin Tickets:** 3632
* **Total Tech Tickets:** 2955
* **Average Monthly Charges:** 64.76

---

##  Dashboard Insights

###  Churn Analysis
* Overall churn rate visualization
* Identification of high-risk customer segments
* Clear comparison between churned and retained customers

---

###  Churn by Payment Method
* Analysis of churn rate across:
  * Electronic check
  * Mailed check
  * Bank transfer
  * Credit card
* Helps identify risky payment behaviors
---

### 🌐 Internet Service Distribution
* Customer segmentation by service type:
  * Fiber optic
  * DSL
  * No internet
* Highlights which services have higher churn rates
---

###  Revenue Insights
* Distribution of total and monthly charges
* Impact of pricing on churn behavior

---

###  Contract Analysis

* Churn rate based on contract type:
  * Month-to-month
  * One-year
  * Two-year
* Shows that shorter contracts typically have higher churn risk
---

###  Customer Segmentation
* Gender-based churn comparison
* Senior citizen percentage
* Partner vs non-partner analysis
---

##  Key Insights
* Customers with **month-to-month contracts** show higher churn
* **Fiber optic users** tend to have higher churn rates
* Certain **payment methods** correlate with increased churn risk
* Higher monthly charges may contribute to churn
